Excited to announce I am working with lowRISC C.I.C. this summer in their Cambridge office!
lowRISC are a non-profit who act as stewards for several open-source semiconductor projects. I encountered some of their designs while working on my final-year college project and, inspired by their goals, contacted them to look for a chance to contribute.
While I have had a fantastic time at AMD, I'm very excited by the learning opportunities presented working at a smaller company, and looking forward to making my contribution to the project.
